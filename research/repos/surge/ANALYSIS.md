# Surge Analysis

1. What problem does it solve?

Surge is framed by its authors as "an AI coding standard kit for Drupal development" that gives AI tools enough Drupal-specific context to work with a codebase "with Drupal, not around it," by combining discoverable skills, open standards, and project/environment guidance from day one. It is presented as a flexible foundation rather than a closed product, with both skill aggregation and generated `AGENTS.md` guidance for a consuming Drupal project. 6. Naming convention.

Skill names are lowercase, hyphen-separated directory names whose directory basename must match the `name` in `SKILL.md`; examples in the docs are `my-skill` and `drupal-agents-generator`. Surge does not enforce ai_best_practices' gerund-prefix rule; it enforces lowercase, allowed characters, hyphen rules, and directory-name parity only.
